About AayushAI

AayushAI is an innovative platform developed by Pagadala Vivek (21R91A7348), Kunta Varun Goud (21R91A7325), and Arshanapally Manideep (21R91A7303) from the A3 batch of section AIML, fourth year, Teegala Krishna Reddy Engineering College, under the guidance of Dr. P. Jyothi, Assistant Professor. The name AayushAI is inspired by the Sanskrit word Aayush, meaning "life" or "longevity," symbolizing the platform's goal to enhance health outcomes through artificial intelligence. Just as life depends on breathing, with lungs as essential organs, this AI tool aims to extend patients' lives by detecting cancerous tumors early in CT scans, thus improving the chance for timely intervention and increasing the "Aayush" (life span) of patients. AayushAI leverages deep learning to analyze chest CT scans, providing rapid and accurate assessments for the early detection of adenocarcinoma, a type of lung cancer. Our mission is to empower patients and healthcare providers with advanced technology to aid in early diagnosis, ultimately improving survival rates. While AayushAI supports the diagnostic process, it is not a substitute for professional medical advice, and patients should always consult their healthcare providers for a complete diagnosis and treatment plan.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can this tool replace a medical diagnosis?

    No, this tool is intended for educational and preliminary assessment purposes. It is essential to consult with a qualified healthcare professional for a complete diagnosis and treatment plan.

  • What should I do if my scan is classified as potentially cancerous?

    If the analysis indicates a potential concern, it is crucial to schedule an appointment with your doctor or a lung specialist immediately. They will conduct further tests and examinations to confirm the diagnosis and determine the best course of action.

  • What types of CT scans are compatible with this tool?

    This tool is designed to analyze standard chest CT scans. If you have any questions about the compatibility of your specific scan, please consult with our support team or your healthcare provider.

  • How accurate is the AI analysis?

    Our AI model has been trained on a large dataset of CT scans and has demonstrated high accuracy in detecting adenocarcinoma. However, it's important to remember that no AI system is perfect. Always consult with your doctor for a definitive diagnosis.

  • Is my data secure?

    Yes, your data privacy is our top priority. We use secure servers and encryption to protect your information.  No personal or medical data is stored after the analysis is complete.
